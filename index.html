Meet me

Nicholas Nganga is a passionate problem solver with a rich background in mechanical, electrical, and computer knowledge, having attained a degree in Mechatronics Engineering from Dedan Kimathi University of Technology. He is passionate about robotics, AI, and scalable software system design, with hands-on experience in control systems, signal processing, and currently pursuing backend development through ALX. Nicholas has applied these skills to impactful real-world projects, including robotics prototypes and financial system deployments. Having competed at the Kenya WorldSkills Competition 2023 and the National Robotics Dojo 2024 and won, he has earned recognition and certificates that reflect his technical expertise and competitive edge. Beyond competitions, Nicholas is known for his collaborative leadership, mentoring fellow students, and driving projects from concept to execution. Deeply curious about how technology can solve everyday problems, he aspires to develop robotics and AI solutions that advance industrial automation and improve quality of life. When not engineering, Nicholas enjoys sharing knowledge, exploring new innovations, and tackling challenges that push him to think differently.
